About

I am a results-driven Digital Marketing Specialist and Web Developer based in Lahore, Pakistan. As Social Media Manager at 7Scribes since 2023, I have scaled social media presence across Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter), delivering a 35% follower growth in just six months through strategic content planning, high-engagement creatives, and targeted advertising.

I combine creative execution with data-driven precision — developing content calendars, producing graphics and reels using Canva and Filmora, and optimizing performance with Meta Business Suite and Google Analytics to achieve measurable ROI.

Complementing my marketing expertise, I am proficient in MERN Stack web development. I am currently pursuing a BSIT (expected 2027) and hold certifications from DigiSkill.pk, LinkedIn, and HubSpot Academy.
